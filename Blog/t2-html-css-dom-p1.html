What is meant by boxifying design?

Boxifying design means to look at a design in terms of boxes, and create different sections using boxes, to help give it structure.

What is the box model?

The box model applys to all HTML elements, which are essentially all boxes. There are four sections to the box, th content, padding, border and then the margin. Each play their own role in the design of an HTML element, and can be customised.
