How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?
These are three types of happiness, that vary in sustainability. The pleasure type of happiness is more difficult to sustain, and is about constant moving from one thing that makes you happy to another.If you only concentrate on the big things that make you happy, you may miss out on the smaller good things. The second type is Passion. Passion as a type of happiness is more consistent, and has a flow pattern that allows you to always enjoy the small things. The last type is Higher Purpose, which is more about understanding the happiness in your surroundings, and having that reflect on you. This seems to be the ideal type of happiness, and is described as being resilient and lasting a long time.
